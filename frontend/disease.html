DISEASE SOLUTIONS

EARLY BLIGHT

Tomatoes that have early blight require immediate attention before the disease takes over the plants. Thoroughly spray the plant (bottoms of leaves also) with Bonide Liquid Copper Fungicide concentrate or Bonide Tomato & Vegetable. Both of these treatments are organic.

CURL

Applying copper oxychloride or lime sulphur sprays as above, or Bordeaux mixture. Bagging and binning any affected leaves or fruit. Hygiene is important - clean up any leaf, branch or fruit material that accumulates beneath the tree. Spores can overwinter in these materials, reinfecting the tree in spring.
